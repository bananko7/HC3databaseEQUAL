Why are most plastic grocery bags white ? White is the default color for plastic bags , why is this ? is this the natural color of the plastic & hence the cheapest color to produce ? Please explain like I'm five.<>Plastic bags can be produced easily in almost any color . White is common because it is the natural color of the base material , and easy to print on . Adding a color is very cheap but makes it harder to see a printed logo .<>Plastic grocery bags are often white because it is the cheapest color to produce. The natural color of plastic is actually a milky white color, so manufacturers don't have to use any additional dyes to make the bags white. Using dyes to color plastic can add to the cost of production, so by using the natural color of the plastic, manufacturers can save money and pass those savings on to their customers.<>reddit_eli5
