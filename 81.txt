Some possible causes include:\n\nMuscular Dystrophy: This is a group of inherited disorders that cause muscle weakness and loss of muscle mass.\n\nSpinal Cord Injury: An injury to the spinal cord can cause weakness in the legs.\n\nCerebral Palsy: This is a neurological disorder that can cause muscle weakness and difficulty with movement.\n\nNeuromuscular disorders: Disorders that affect the nerves and muscles, such as Charcot-Marie-Tooth disease, can cause weakness in the legs.\n\nInfections: Certain infections, such as polio or West Nile virus, can cause weakness in the legs.\n\nIt is important to have your nephew evaluated by a doctor to determine the cause of his leg weakness.
